About

The person
behind the work.

"I build things that work silently in the background — and occasionally ride a Kawasaki ZX-6R too fast on an empty highway."

Dark Ink is a sole proprietorship built at the intersection of systems thinking and craft. By day, I run IT and operations at a fintech company — identity management, Google Workspace, infrastructure, the unglamorous work that keeps everything else running.

I studied Economics at the University of Utah, which is really just a degree in "how do incentives, constraints, and information produce outcomes" — turns out that's surprisingly useful when designing automation for real businesses.

The consulting and the streams are the same thing at their core: a preference for doing things properly, even when no one is watching. Especially when no one is watching.
